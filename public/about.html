About Us

Quality

Marryme is the first and most secure site in Israel that allows you to choose from a huge variety of rings depending on the style, design and budget.

Price

As international manufacturers and marketers and members of the Diamond Exchange, you are guaranteed a reasonable price for spectacular jewelry - with no overheads and no brokerage gaps.

Just for you

A purchase on the site is a purchase with open eyes. Here you will find original jewelry decorated with high quality white diamonds that have been hand-picked especially for you and decorated at the artist level by us.

Know what you are buying

The diamonds on the site are accompanied by an international gemological certificate confirming the originality and quality of the diamond. We have been members of the Diamond Exchange for 35 years and operate a boutique store within the Exchange. That is, you purchase jewelry from a reputable, experienced and reputable body and with a warranty. Moreover, at "MarryMe", the jewelry is made entirely by us: from the original model, casting, inlay and luxury packaging. This is how you get an original, high-quality piece of jewelry that is produced in a controlled process. The variety of styles will allow you to choose between classic, modern, decorated and rich in diamonds or minimalist designs.
